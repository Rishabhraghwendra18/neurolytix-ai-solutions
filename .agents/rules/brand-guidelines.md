# Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette

- **Primary (Deep Tech):** `#0F172A` (Midnight Navy)  
  → Used for backgrounds

- **Accent 1 (AI Pulse):** `#38BDF8` (Electric Cyan)  
  → Used for links and icons

- **Accent 2 (Logic Purple):** `#818CF8` (Soft Indigo)  
  → Used for gradients and buttons

- **Text:** `#F8FAFC` (Off-white)  
  → Ensures readability on dark backgrounds

---

## Typography

- **Headings:**  
  *Inter* or *Bento* (Bold, 700–800 weight)  
  → Clean, geometric, modern look

- **Body Text:**  
  *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Regular, 400 weight)  
  → High readability for technical content

---

## Visual Style

- Use **Glassmorphism**  
  → Semi-transparent, blurred backgrounds for cards

- Incorporate **Mesh Gradients**  
  → Especially in hero sections to simulate *“neural energy”*

- **Icons Style:**  
  → Thin-line or 3D isometric for a cutting-edge feel

---

## Brand Voice

- Professional  
- Visionary  
- Results-driven  

**Focus on:**
- Scalability  
- Intelligence  
- Customization  

**Avoid:**
- Fluff or vague language
